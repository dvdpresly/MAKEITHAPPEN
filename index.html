Whole Commercial Layer Chickens

Our laying flock, sourced from world-class genetics, delivers consistent excellence starting from Point of Lay at 18-20 weeks. These hens are expertly managed to sustain a peak production rate above 90%, resulting in reliable volume and over 300 eggs per cycle. We guarantee superior quality, including strong shells, a rich, vibrant yolk color, and ideal sizing, ensuring every egg meets the high standards your business demands for freshness and market appeal.

Transitioning from their highly productive laying cycle at $\approx 72-78$ weeks, our ex-layers offer a premium, flavorful meat option. Unlike young broilers, these mature hens possess lean, robust muscle tissue, making them the ideal choice for creating rich, savory stocks, authentic bone broths, and slow-cooked dishes. Choosing our spent hens is a sustainable and economical choice for consumers seeking depth of flavor and culinary tradition.
